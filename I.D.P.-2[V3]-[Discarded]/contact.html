Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation and follow the simple registration process. You'll need to provide your mobile number and create a password.

Is JobPortal free to use?

Yes, JobPortal is completely free for job seekers. Employers can post basic job listings for free with optional premium features available.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Log In" and then "Forgot Password." You'll receive instructions on your registered mobile number to reset your password.

Can I apply for jobs without an account?

While you can browse jobs without an account, you'll need to create a free account to apply for positions and save your applications.
